Flavors

KitKat Chunky Bar

The classic taste offers a delightful break in every bite, combining the smoothness of milk chocolate with a satisfyingly crispy wafer. It's a timeless snack that brings a simple joy to your taste buds, perfect for those moments when only the comforting classics will do.

KitKat Chunky Popcorn Bar

This innovative twist brings a playful crunch to the beloved chocolate treat. With bits of real popcorn embedded within the chocolate, it offers a unique texture and a slightly salty kick that contrasts beautifully with the sweet milk chocolate, creating an unexpectedly delightful munching experience.

KitKat Chunky Peanut Butter

This version takes the indulgence to another level, blending the creamy, savory richness of peanut butter with the classic crunch of KitKat. The combination of smooth peanut butter and chocolate with the crispy wafer offers a mouth-watering treat that satisfies both sweet and savory cravings in every chunky bite.
